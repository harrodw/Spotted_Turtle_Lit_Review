A liturature review and meta-analysis of spotted turtle (Clemmys guttata) home range sizes across North America. 
We are interested in questions such as differences between sexes, HR estimators, and ecoregions.

Authors: Abigail Conklin, Eliza Foli, Will Harrod, Courtney Grubbs, Alex Acker, Charlotte Whorton, & Ivana Mali
